HOW TO VISIT THE PYRAMIDS IN EGYPT

The Pyramids of Giza are one of the most iconic landmarks in the world. Located in Egypt, they are a must-visit for any traveler. Here are some tips on how to visit the pyramids:

  • Hire a local guide to learn about the history and significance of the pyramids.
  • Wear comfortable shoes and bring plenty of water.
  • Don't forget to visit the Sphinx, which is located nearby.
